Meet The Founder

Hello, I'm Samantha

Welcome to NovaWorx SA Services — a virtual business support practice built on three core pillars: Professionalism, Reliability, and Confidentiality.

As a dedicated Virtual Assistant and Founder, I created NovaWorx with one goal in mind: to give business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals the gift of time. Time to focus on what you do best, while I handle the operational details that keep your business running smoothly.

With a keen eye for detail, strong organisational skills, and a genuine commitment to client success, I bring a personalised approach to every task. Whether you need ongoing administrative support, inbox management, customer communication, or document organisation — I'm here to help.

I understand that trusting someone with your business operations requires confidence. That's why discretion and confidentiality are at the heart of every engagement with NovaWorx SA.
